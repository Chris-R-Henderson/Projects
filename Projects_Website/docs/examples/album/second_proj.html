This is a thrust vector proof of concept project that was designed to attempt to achieve hovering flight using a gimble and monopropeller. This project came from a desire to design a thrust vectoring system for the CSULB Beach Launch Team rocket. The main focus of the project was to figure out a flight controller that would work in conjunction with two accelerometers to detect the changing center of gravity as the rocket burns its fuel. Center of gravity on a rocket is an important variable in solving the problem of directing thust as needed to stabilize the rocket in flight. I worked on this project with Brandon Cavanaugh and I would like to acknowledge his work as this project was his idea and will likely be completed by him while I work on the Pubworks engine that is system will ultimately be used on.
