This project is a vacuum tube guitar amp that is based on a 1957 Fender 5e3 circuit. This amp produces 12 watts with with 6v6 power tubes and 12ax7 preamp tubes. I purchased the cab and blank chassis as a kit and wiried the amp by hand. I also incorporated some modifications to the basic 5e3 circuit that includes a treble cut and a channel split that allows the mixing of the bright and fat channels. This project was completed Jan 2019 and I still use it for guitar practice.
